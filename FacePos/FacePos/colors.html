Bootstrap colors

These are the primary colors used throughout various Bootstrap controls. They've been altered to use Windows 10 colors instead of Bootstrap colors.

$brand-primary: $win-color-accent !default;

$brand-success: $win-color-core-green !default;

$brand-info: $win-color-secondary-blue !default;

$brand-warning: $win-color-core-yellow !default;

$brand-danger: $win-color-core-red !default;
